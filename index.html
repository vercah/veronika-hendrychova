About me

I'm an engineering student at the Faculty of Nuclear Sciences and Physical Engineering of the Czech Technical University in Prague, Czechia. This year I study Computer Science at the Université de Rennes in France as an Erasmus exchange student. Recently I have published my first paper in the field of Combinatorics on words with the results from my bachelor thesis.

Apart from studying I'm an active member of the FYKOS student group. I'm engaged primarily in event management and IT. We organize several physics competitions for high-school students each year, including the Physics Correspondence Competition, Fyziklani (in-presence physics competition in Prague), Physics Brawl Online (online version of Fyziklani) and Day with Experimental Physics.

I also enjoy contributing to other educational events for students and children, such as Science Week at the FNSPE CTU, or Science summer camps for children organized by the Faculty of Science, Charles University.
