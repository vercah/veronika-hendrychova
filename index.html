About me

I'm an engineering student at the Czech Technical University in Prague, Faculty of Nuclear Sciences and Physical Engineering. Currently, I study the second year of Masters in Mathematical Computer Science, where I also work as a teaching assistant in Linear algebra. I spent the last year in France as an Erasmus exchange student at the ENS Rennes.

Apart from studying, I'm an active member of the FYKOS student group. I'm engaged primarily in event management and IT. We organize several physics competitions for high-school students each year, including the FYKOS Competition, Fyziklani (in-person physics competition in Prague), Physics Brawl Online (online version of Fyziklani), and Day with Experimental Physics.

I also enjoy contributing to other educational events for students and children, such as the PAIR Camp, or Science summer camps organized by the Faculty of Science, Charles University. I find joy in learning foreign languages, playing Go, and practicing piano play.
