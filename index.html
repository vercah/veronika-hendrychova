About me

I study Mathematical Computer Science master's program at the Czech Technical University in Prague, Faculty of Nuclear Sciences and Physical Engineering. I also work there as a teaching assistant in Linear Algebra. Currently, I am exploring PhD positions in bioinformatics, particularly with some overlap with mathematics or modeling.

Apart from studying, I like to popularize science among high-school students, and I take part organizing both nation-wide and international competitions and events as a member of the FYKOS student group. I'm engaged primarily in event management and IT. We organize several physics competitions for high-school students each year, including the FYKOS Competition, Fyziklani (in-person physics competition in Prague), Physics Brawl Online (online version of Fyziklani), and Day with Experimental Physics.

I also enjoy contributing to other educational events for students and children, such as the PAIR Camp, or Science summer camps organized by the Faculty of Science, Charles University. I find joy in learning foreign languages, playing Go, attempting calisthenics, and practicing piano play.
